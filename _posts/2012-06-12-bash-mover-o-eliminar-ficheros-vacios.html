--- title: 'Bash: Mover o eliminar ficheros vacios de un directorio' date: '2012-06-12T12:27:00.001+02:00' tags: modified_time: '2012-06-12T12:34:43.413+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-5062930559472516636.post-4188627021807101174 blogger_orig_url: http://sk-forgetmenot.blogspot.com/2012/06/bash-mover-o-eliminar-ficheros-vacios.html --- Mover todos los ficheros vacios de un directorio al subdirectorio empty {% highlight bash %} ls -la | awk '{if ($5==0) print $9}' | xargs mv -t ./empty/ {% endhighlight %} Para eliminarlos basta con {% highlight bash %} ls -la | awk '{if ($5==0) print $9}' | xargs rm {% endhighlight %}
